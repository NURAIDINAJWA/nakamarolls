Our Story began


The Nakama Rolls was born out of the passion of four friends who wished to bring home Goi cuon inspired by their love towards Asian food especially from Vietnam.

Touched by the taste, flavors and experience eating Vietnam food, the ladies realized that there was no one specific hip space or neighbourhood in the Selangor City.

They opened Nakama Rolls Cafe in 2022, away from the bustling crowd, commencing a new chapter in Shah Alam cafe history. The cafe presented different flavours of Vietnamese Rolls.

With the location, they aimed to create a new happening spot in one of Johor Bahru sleepy corners. More than 10 years later and with continued success, this must indeed come true.
